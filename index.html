No. 01 — About Me

Bonjour, Hello

Emily Woo is a personal stylist based in Paris. Her style philosophy combines a love for vintage and color without compromising the full utilization of one's wardrobe.

Personal style is a reflection of who you are, and it should evolve with you throughout your life. Emily's stylist services help you maximize your existing wardrobe, take the drama out of shopping for new pieces, and teach you how to embrace and embody your personal style.
